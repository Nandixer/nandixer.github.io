Sarah the Nerfkitten Guide

Back

Sarah the Nerfkitten is the newest Event Boss created by Yoshino.

To get there, /join yoshino.

Notable abilities:

  • dodges all avoidable attacks
  • applies a “Nerf” debuff that stacks infinitely and lowers damage.
  • all damage done to Sarah is capped at 10 000

Shaman

Shaman or Evolved Shaman can loop Elemental Embrace, negating Sarah’s dodge, allowing the entire 20-player team to damage her.

Buffers

In a team of 20 people, you should have at least one Legion Revenant, StoneCrusher, Lord of Order, and LightCaster. The goal is to make sure everyone is healed regularly, is at max haste, and regularly hits the 10 000 damage cap.

Damagers

If the roles of Shaman and Buffers are taken care of, your best best is to play a damage role. Because of the damage cap, you want frequent hits, not high hits.

Your best bet is:

  • a class with multiple DoTs like Infinity Knight
  • a class with spammable skills like ArchFiend
  • a class with a low-cooldown Auto-Attack like Chrono Assassin or ShadowStalker of Time
    • take Health Vamp for one additional hit
